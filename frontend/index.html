How it works

From the world's news to your team's briefing

Three steps, completely automated. You just read.

01

We watch the news so you don't have to

Every few hours, NexusBrief silently scans hundreds of publications, blogs, and news outlets across your chosen industries. New stories are identified, duplicates are discarded, and only the most relevant content makes it through — without you lifting a finger.

Automatic
02

Every story becomes a sharp, usable briefing

Each article is analyzed by our AI engine, which extracts the core message, distills it into a crisp executive summary, pulls out the three most important points, and assigns a sentiment score. The result reads in under a minute.

AI-powered
03

Your team reads, filters, and acts — in minutes

Open your dashboard to a curated feed of everything that happened. Filter by topic or sentiment. Click any story to open a full briefing panel. If you need the full article, one click takes you there.
